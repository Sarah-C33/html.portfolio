The Best Movies for Sarah

My Top 3 Movies

Ying Yang Master

Great chinese fantasy period movie with misunderstood protagonist who
saves everyone in the end.

The 300

An old favourite but it has not aged at all,
it tells a story of spartans who fight against invadors
even though they are extremely outnumbered.

My Sisters Keeper

A tear jerker and even older than the The 300,
it tells the story of a young girl who was essentially a designer baby so that she could be used for stem cells, undergoing painful procedures
to treat her older sister who has luekemia.
